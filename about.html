    My Story

    I am a secondary education teacher turned UX designer currently living in New York. I am looking for new challenges that will help me grow as a professional in the field of UX design. With over sixteen years of experience as an educator, I have had the opportunity to work with a vast number of individuals from all different backgrounds. Getting to know my students and planning lessons according to their needs is a strong suit of mine and I believe this skill will help me further develop my abilities in the world of UX design. I am a good listener and can empathize with future clients in order to design what they need to reach their goals. While I am not working as a teacher or creating new designs, I enjoy traveling the world and studying languages. I love getting to know new places and communicating with others in their native language.
