The Home Expert App

Project Overview

The Home Expert App is an app designed to help users complete their home improvement/home care projects. The Home Expert App appeals to users who are very hands-on and want to complete their own home projects, users who need a little creative inspiration, as well as users who want the ease of hiring someone to complete a task for them.

  • Purpose:

    To connect users to the information they need to carry out home projects and to connect users to local experts who can carry out projects for them.

  • Objective:

    To provide users with a way to access information about home care projects. To connect users with expert home repair specialists in their area.

  • Role:

    Lead UX designer, UX researcher

  • Duration of the project:

    Approximately 3 months

Stages of the Project

  1. Defining the Problem

    In this stage, I developed the following problem statement that I would use to guide my work: Our home project enthusiasts need a way to contact experts and seek advice on home projects because they wish to fix, repair, replace, improve, or redesign some element of their home. We will know this to be true when we see how many users are using our app to research information about home improvement projects.

  2. Competitive Analysis

    I researched several different apps on the market to see what I thought worked best with each and what I thought could use some improvement.

  3. Business Requirements

    The goal of this was to align all stakeholders on what our product does, who it is designed for, and what requirements are necessary to successfully build our product.

  4. User research

    I conducted user interviews and user surveys to help gain insight into what users are looking for in a home improvement app and what they thought was missing in the world of home improvement apps.

  5. User Personas

    I created three user personas based on the research I had done up until this point. The user personas represented the different types of users that would possibly interact with the app. Having the user personas in mind while designing the app helped me focus on what their needs and wants might be.
